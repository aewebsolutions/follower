Basic

Make an element to follow scrolling

Events

Make an element to follow scrolling


$('#demo-5').follower();

$('#demo-5').on('followerGetTop', function(){
    console.log('#demo-5 has reached the top')
});
$('#demo-5').on('followerGetBottom', function(){
    console.log('#demo-5 has reached the bottom')
});
Centered Content
